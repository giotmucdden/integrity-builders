# Integrity Builders — Design Brainstorm

## Approach A — Industrial Precision
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Industrial Modernism meets Blueprint Drafting
**Core Principles:** Raw strength, precision craftsmanship, structural clarity, bold confidence
**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#1a1a1a) backgrounds with lime/construction green (#4ade80) as the primary accent, warm white text, and red badge highlights — mirroring the actual brand logo palette
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed hero with diagonal section cuts, asymmetric two-column service grid, sticky header with transparent-to-solid scroll transition
**Signature Elements:** Diagonal CSS clip-path section dividers, construction-tape yellow/green accent lines, blueprint-style grid texture overlays
**Interaction Philosophy:** Mechanical precision — buttons feel like pressing a physical switch, hover states reveal information with a "slide-in" motion
**Animation:** Entrance animations triggered on scroll (fade-up), counter animations for stats, smooth carousel transitions for gallery
**Typography System:** Oswald (bold, condensed) for headings — industrial, strong; Lato for body — clean, readable
</text>

## Approach C — Premium Green & Black Craftsmanship ✅ CHOSEN
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Premium Trade Craftsmanship — sophisticated yet grounded
**Core Principles:** Brand-faithful (green/black/red), premium feel, strong trust signals, clean hierarchy
**Color Philosophy:** Jet black (#111111) as the base, brand green (#3cb043 / lime-600) as the hero accent, crisp white for content sections, red (#dc2626) for badges and emphasis — directly from the logo
**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric hero with logo prominently placed, horizontally auto-scrolling photo strip, card-based services grid with icon accents, clean booking form section with calendar
**Signature Elements:** Green left-border accent lines on section titles, subtle dark texture on hero, badge-style "Licensed · Insured · Bonded" trust strip
**Interaction Philosophy:** Confident and smooth — hover lifts on cards, green glow on CTAs, calendar dates highlight in brand green
**Animation:** Scroll-triggered fade-up for sections, infinite auto-scroll for photo gallery, smooth form transitions
**Typography System:** Bebas Neue (display) for hero headings — bold and industrial; Nunito Sans for body — warm and readable
</text>
